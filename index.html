Biography

Zhiyao Ma is a Ph.D student in the department of computer science at Yale University. He is advised by Professor Lin Zhong, Ph.D. His research interests include operating system, embedded system, programming language and cloud computing. He enjoys driving on tracks at HPDE, currently in the intermediate run group, and holds a Kendo 3rd Dan rank.
